From Zero to Paper 🚗🧠

The whole journey of a real research project, step by step and in plain language: from driving data to a neural network, from NAS to running the model on an STM32 chip — and, at the end, a paper for a reputable journal.

The course's golden rule 📏

Everything you read here really happened in this very repository: the code is there, the results are logged (logs/experiments.jsonl), and it is reproducible. There are no made-up numbers.
