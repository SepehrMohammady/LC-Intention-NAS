# Venue analysis

Researched 2026-07-07. Decision pending with user/supervisor.

## IEEE Signal Processing Letters (user's initial candidate)

- Q1 (Scimago Signal Processing), IF 4.63 (2024 JCR). Single-blind,
  ~3-week nominal review cycle, ~2–3 months to decision. Free (non-OA).
- **Hard limit: 4 pages technical content + 1 page references-only.**
- Fit: ML-for-signal-processing EDICS covers NAS on 1D signals; the
  **baseline paper itself is in SPL** (vol. 33, 2026) — a letter that beats
  it on the same dataset/boards is a natural fit and easy for reviewers to
  assess. Risk: 4 pages force a very condensed deployment story.

## Recommendation to discuss

- **SPL** if we want speed + the head-to-head "we beat an SPL letter in SPL"
  story and can fit 4 pages (method fig + one results table + one deployment
  table is doable).
- **IEEE IoT Journal** if the deployment/NAS engineering story should breathe
  (8 pages) with higher IF and still-fast review.

Suggested default: draft compact (SPL discipline), decide after first NAS
results — content volume will make the choice obvious.
